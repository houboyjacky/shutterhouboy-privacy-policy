Thank you for using the Shutter Mystic Domain service (the "Service"). To protect your rights and ensure transparency in personal data practices, we hereby explain how your personal data is collected, processed, and used in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

---

## 1. Purpose of Collection and Use
- To provide astrology, Four Pillars (Bazi), and related interpretation services.
- To deliver personalized analysis, responses, and feature recommendations based on data you provide.
- To maintain service operations, system security, quality control, statistical analysis, and troubleshooting.
- To conduct language model research, testing, and optimization within a lawful and necessary scope to improve service quality.
- To comply with lawful requests from competent authorities or judicial agencies.

---

## 2. Types of Data Collected
- Basic identification data: name, nickname, contact details, and account identifiers.
- Astrology-related data: date of birth, time of birth, place of birth, birth chart, or other information required for astrology analysis.
- Interaction and content data: questions you submit, feedback, uploaded content, and system response records.
- System log data: access or usage timestamps, device and operation records, error logs, and security event records.
- Other data voluntarily provided by you and relevant to the Service purpose.

---

## 3. Scope and Method of Use
- Personal data will be processed through automated or semi-automated methods only to the extent necessary for the stated purposes.
- Where necessary, the Service may engage service providers for system maintenance, cloud hosting, analytics, or customer support, and will require them to comply with confidentiality and information security obligations under contracts and applicable laws.
- Unless otherwise required by law, by lawful authority request, or with your separate consent, your data will not be used for purposes unrelated to the original collection purpose.

---

## 4. Consent for Language Model Research Use
- You understand that the Service may use de-identified, anonymized, or minimized data for language model research, evaluation, testing, and quality improvement.
- If additional consent is required for specific data uses, the Service will provide clear notice and obtain your consent before such processing.
- You may request at any time to stop using newly collected data for research purposes; however, lawful processing completed before the effective date of your request will remain unaffected.

---

## 5. Retention and Deletion Mechanism
- Personal data will be retained only for the period necessary to fulfill the collection purposes or as required by applicable laws.
- When the purpose no longer exists, the retention period expires, or you submit a lawful deletion request that meets legal requirements, the Service will, within a reasonable period, delete the data, cease processing and use, or apply de-identification.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Contact Information
Subject to applicable data protection laws, you may exercise the following rights regarding your personal data:
- Request inquiry or access.
- Request a copy.
- Request supplementation or correction.
- Request cessation of collection, processing, or use.
- Request deletion.

To exercise these rights, please contact us through the customer service or contact channels announced by the Service. The Service may perform necessary identity verification and will respond within a reasonable period.

---

## 7. Data Security and Cross-Border Transfer
- The Service adopts reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
- If cross-border transfer is necessary for operations, it will be conducted in compliance with applicable laws and with appropriate safeguards.

---

## 8. Updates to This Notice
This Notice may be updated due to legal changes, service adjustments, or operational needs. Updated versions will be published on relevant Service pages and become effective upon publication.
